Jieba
“Jieba” (Chinese for “to stutter”) Chinese text segmentation: built to be the best Python Chinese word segmentation module.

Features
Supports three tokenization modes:
Exact mode, which tries to cut the sentence most accurately, suitable for text analysis;
Full mode, which scans all the words that can be formed into words in the sentence, it is very fast, but it cannot resolve the ambiguity;
The search engine mode, based on the precise mode, cuts long words again and improves the recall rate.
Support traditional word segmentation
Support custom dictionary
